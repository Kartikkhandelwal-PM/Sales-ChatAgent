# ExpressITR || Pass Through Income Details (Schedule PTI)

**Section:** Data Feeding

**Category:** Express-ITR

**Source:** https://support.kdksoftware.com/portal/en/kb/articles/pass-through-income-details-schedule-pti

**Tags:** pass through income data feeding, schedule pti, pass through income data, pass through income, pass through income details, schedule pti data feeding, expressitr

---

To feed Pass Through Income (Schedule PTI) details in Express ITR, follow these steps:

Step 1: Access the ITR Summary

Open the client profile in the Express ITR application.

In the ITR Summary section, click on Return Flow to proceed.

      

Step 2: Open Schedule PTI

Navigate to Schedule PTI under the Head of Income.

      

Step 3: Enter Business Trust/Investment Fund Information

Fill in the Name of Business Trust / Investment Fund, PAN, and Section (choose either 115UA Business Trust or 115UB Investment Trust as applicable).

      

Step 4: Input Pass Through Income Details

Enter the specific details for the Pass Through Income (PTI).

      

By following these steps, you can efficiently record PTI details for clients in Express ITR.
