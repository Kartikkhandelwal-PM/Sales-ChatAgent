Agricultural Income Data Entry in Express ITR

To record Agricultural Income for a client in Express ITR, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Access the Return Flow

Open the client profile in Express ITR.

In the ITR Summary section, click on Return Flow to proceed.

      

Step 2: Navigate to Exempt Income Head

Locate the Exempt Income Head where agricultural income entries are made.

      

Step 3: Enter Agricultural Income Details

Fill in the agricultural income details, such as Receipt and Expenditure.

      
If Agricultural Income is less than ₹5,00,000:

Enter only the income-related details (e.g., Receipt, Expenditure).

      

If Agricultural Income exceeds ₹5,00,000:

Additional land details are required, including:

Name of District

PIN Code

Measurement of Agricultural Land (in Acres)

Land Type (e.g., irrigated or non-irrigated)

Land Status (owned or leased)

      

By following these steps, you can accurately enter agricultural income for your client in Express ITR.
