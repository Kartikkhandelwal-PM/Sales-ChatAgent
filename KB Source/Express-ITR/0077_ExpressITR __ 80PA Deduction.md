Process to Feed 80PA Deduction Details for Producer Companies

Applicability of Section 80PA

Eligible Business: Section 80PA allows a 100% deduction of profits and gains attributable to the eligible business of a producer company.

Turnover Limit: The turnover of the producer company must be below ₹100 crore in the previous financial year.

Steps to Feed 80PA Deduction in Express ITR

Step 1: Access the ITR Summary

Log in to Express ITR using your credentials and open the profile of the producer company.
Navigate to the ITR Summary section and click on Return Flow to start the process.

Step 2: Provide Business Information

Open the Business Profession head and navigate to the Business Information section.

      

Step 3: Update Organization Info

Go to the Organization Info section.

Select "Yes" for the question, "Are you a Producer Company as defined in Sec 581A of Companies Act?"

      
Step 4: Open the Deductions Head

Navigate to the Deductions Head in the software.

      

Step 5: Enter 80PA Deduction Details

Locate the section 80PA: Deduction in respect of certain income of producer companies.

Feed the relevant deduction details based on the profits and gains attributable to the eligible business of the producer company.

       

Note: Ensure the deduction details are accurate and supported by the financial statements and turnover records to comply with the eligibility criteria.

This streamlined process allows you to properly claim deductions under Section 80PA, ensuring tax benefits for eligible producer companies.
