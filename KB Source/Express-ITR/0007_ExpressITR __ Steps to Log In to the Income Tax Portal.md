Steps to Log In to the Income Tax Portal

Launch the Software: Open and log in to the designated ExpressITR Application.

Select the Client: Navigate to the Client Master and choose the relevant client.

Access the Portal: Click on the Income Tax Department logo to initiate the login process.

The software will automatically redirect you to the Income Tax Portal for authentication and login.
