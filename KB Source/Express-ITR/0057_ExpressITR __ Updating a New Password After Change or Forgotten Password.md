Updating a New Password After Change or Forgotten Password

Step 1: Access the Client Profile

Open the client profile for which you need to update the password.

Click on the Sync button or Login at ITD button. 

If the password has changed, you will be prompted to update it.

      

Step 2: Enter and Save the New Password

Enter the new password in the provided field.

Click on Update Now & Continue to save the changes.

      

Following these steps will help you quickly update a new password in Express ITR after a password change or reset.
