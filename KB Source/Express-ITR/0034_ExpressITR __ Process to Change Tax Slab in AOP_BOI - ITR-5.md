Process to Change Tax Slab in AOP/BOI ITR-5

This guide provides a step-by-step process to change the tax slab for an Association of Persons (AOP) or Body of Individuals (BOI) while filing ITR-5. The applicable tax slab depends on the type of AOP/BOI and the nature of income. Follow the steps below to ensure correct tax slab selection and calculation.

Understand the Tax Slab Categories for AOP/BOI

Category 1: Normal AOP/BOI

Taxed at individual rates based on the income range.

Applicable tax slabs:

      

Additional Charges:

Surcharge:

10% of income tax if income > ₹50 lakhs but ≤ ₹1 crore.

15% of income tax if income > ₹1 crore but ≤ ₹2 crores.

25% of income tax if income > ₹2 crores but ≤ ₹5 crores.

37% of income tax if income > ₹5 crores.

Health and Education Cess: 4% on income tax and surcharge.

Category 2: AOP/BOI with Determinate Shares

Taxed at the Maximum Marginal Rate (MMR) of 30% if the income exceeds the basic exemption limit.

Surcharge and cess apply as mentioned above.

Category 3: AOP/BOI with No Member Exceeding the Basic Exemption Limit

Taxed at individual rates as if each member were an individual.

Category 4: AOP/BOI with Foreign Company Members

Taxed at 40% (plus surcharge and cess).

Steps to Change Tax Slab in ITR-5

Step 1: Access the ITR Profile

Open the client profile in your ExpressITR software.

Navigate to the ITR Summary section.

Click on Return Flow to proceed.

      

Step 2: Open the Business and Profession Head

Locate the Business Profession Head section in the ITR form.

      

Step 3: Enter Member Details

Go to the Business Information tab.

      
Select Member Details and fill in the relevant fields based on the category:
For Determinate Shares:

Enter the percentage of shares held by each member.

Set "Income exceeds max amount not charged to tax" to "No".

                        

                        
For Indeterminate Shares:

Leave the "Percentage of Share Held" field blank.

                        

                        
                        
Step 4: Verify Tax Computation

Open the tax computation section.

Ensure that the tax is calculated as per the applicable rates:

Individual slab rates for normal AOP/BOI.

30% for determinate shares.

40% for foreign company members.

Important Considerations

Losses → Losses of an AOP/BOI cannot be carried forward or set off against member's income.
Alternate Minimum Tax (AMT) → AOP/BOI is subject to AMT at 18.5% of adjusted total income, if applicable.
Cross-Check Filing Details →

Ensure all details entered in the ITR form match the financial data and supporting documents.

Validate the return before submission to avoid errors.
