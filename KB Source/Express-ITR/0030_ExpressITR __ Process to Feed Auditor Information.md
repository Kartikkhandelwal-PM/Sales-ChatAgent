Process to Feed Auditor Information in Express ITR

Step 1: Access Client Profile

Log in to Express ITR and open the profile of the client for whom the audit details need to be updated.
Navigate to the ITR Summary section.

Click on Return Flow to access the relevant ITR options.

      

Step 2: Navigate to Business Profession Head

Locate and click on the Business Profession Head section in the menu.

      
      
Step 3: Enter Auditor Information

Open the Auditor Information section within the Business Profession Head.

      
Ensure that the details match the audit report being filed.

      

Important Notes:

Verify the accuracy of auditor information to avoid discrepancies during submission.

Ensure compliance with all statutory requirements, including audit timelines and report formats.

By following these steps, the auditor’s information will be correctly recorded in the system, ensuring a smooth ITR filing process.
