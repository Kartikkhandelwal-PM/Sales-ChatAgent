House Property Income Entry Process in Express ITR

Here’s a step-by-step guide to entering House Property Income in Express ITR:

Step 1: Access the ITR Summary

Open the client profile in Express ITR.

In the ITR Summary section, select Return Flow to proceed.

      

Step 2: Open the House Property Income Head
Navigate to the House Property Income section to begin entering the relevant details.

      

Step 3: Enter Property-Related Information
Fill in the applicable details, such as:

Property Type

            
Property Details

Tenant Information

Co-ownership Details

Lettable/Actual Rent Received or Receivable Value

Interest Payable on Borrowed Capital

      
Step 4: Co-ownership Status
If the property is co-owned, check the Co-ownership box under Property Details. Then, enter the details for each co-owner as required.

      

By following these steps, you can ensure accurate recording of House Property Income in Express ITR.
