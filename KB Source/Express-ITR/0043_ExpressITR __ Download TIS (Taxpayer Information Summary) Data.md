The Taxpayer
Information Summary (TIS) provides a consolidated view of the taxpayer’s
financial data as available on the Income Tax Department portal. In Express
ITR, users can seamlessly sync, download, and review the TIS data for accurate
return preparation.

Follow the
step-by-step process below:

Step 1: Perform
AIS/TIS Data Synchronization

Before downloading the
TIS, it is essential to fetch the latest data from the Income Tax Department.

Navigate to the ITR Dashboard within
     Express ITR.
Locate and click on the Sync  option.

Initiate the synchronization process to
     retrieve updated AIS (Annual Information Statement) and TIS data.
Note: Ensure that
valid login credentials for the Income Tax portal are configured to
successfully complete the data sync.

Step 2: Download TIS
Summary in PDF Format

Once the
synchronization is successfully completed:

Go to the relevant section where AIS/TIS
     details are displayed.

Select the option to Download TIS PDF Icon.

The system will generate and download the TIS
     Summary in PDF format for your reference.

Step 3: Review TIS
Data in Computation

In addition to the
standalone TIS PDF:

Generate or open the Computation within Express ITR.

The TIS Summary details will be
     incorporated into the computation report.

Carefully review the data to ensure
     accuracy and consistency with the imported information.

Important Points to
Consider
Always perform a fresh AIS/TIS sync before
     downloading the TIS PDF to ensure updated data.
Review discrepancies, if any, between TIS
     and actual records before finalizing the return.
Use the TIS summary as a reference
     document for accurate income reporting and compliance.
