Schedule III Depreciation Chart

The Schedule III Depreciation Chart is applicable only for company clients. Follow these steps to enter the required information:

Step 1: Open the Company Client Profile

Access the company client profile in Express ITR.

Go to the ITR Summary section and click on Return Flow to proceed.

      

Step 2: Navigate to Business Profession Head

Click on Business Profession Income and select Schedule III.

      

      

Step 3: Open Fixed Assets Chart Annexure

Under Schedule III Formats & Notes, click on Fixed Assets Chart Annexure.

            

Step 4: Enter Asset Details

Input all relevant asset information here. The Depreciation as per WDV (Written Down Value) will be automatically calculated.

Be sure to complete mandatory fields, including:

Owned/Leased

Useful Life

Shift

Date of Purchase

Original Cost of Asset

Written Down Value

Following these steps will ensure accurate calculation of depreciation for Schedule III.
