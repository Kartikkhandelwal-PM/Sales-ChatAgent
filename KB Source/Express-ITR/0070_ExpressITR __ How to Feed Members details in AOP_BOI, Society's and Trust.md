How to Feed Member's Details in AOP/BOI, Societies, and Trusts in Express ITR

Follow these steps to enter member's details for Associations of Persons (AOP), Bodies of Individuals (BOI), Societies and Trusts in Express ITR:

Step 1: Access the ITR Summary

Open the client profile in Express ITR.

In the ITR Summary section, click on Return Flow to proceed.

      

Step 2: Open the Business and Profession Head

Navigate to the Business Profession Head section.

      

Step 3: Enter Member Details

Go to Business Information and click on Member Details.

      
Here, you can input all relevant details for each member.

By following these steps, you can efficiently record member's details for AOP, BOI, Societie's and Trusts in Express ITR.
