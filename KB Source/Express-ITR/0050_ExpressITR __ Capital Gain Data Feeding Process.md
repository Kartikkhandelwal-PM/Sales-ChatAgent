Follow these steps to efficiently input capital gain data for a client in the Express ITR application:

Step 1: Access the Client Profile

Open the client’s profile and navigate to the ITR Summary section within the profile.
Click the button under Return Flow to open the relevant ITR options.

      
Step 2: Select Capital Gain Section

Click on the Capital Gain heading to access the capital gain data entry section for this client.

      

Step 3: Open the Appropriate Annexure

In the Capital Gain section, select the appropriate annexure and input the relevant details based on the type of capital asset.

Annexures and Relevant Information

1. Shares (Listed and Unlisted)

ISIN (International Securities Identification Number)

Date of Purchase/Sale

Whether STT Paid (Yes/No)

Fair Market Value (FMV) per share

Other relevant details

      

2. Equity-Linked Mutual Funds

Name of the Mutual Fund

ISIN

Date of Purchase/Sale

FMV per unit

Other relevant details

      

3. Building/Land (Immovable Property)

Type of Property:
Residential House

Urban Agricultural Land

Land/Building/Any Right in Land or Building

Asset Type, Dates, and Details:

Purchase/Sale Date

Deduction/Improvements

Exemptions Claimed

Property and Buyer Details

      

4. Bonds and Debentures

Date of Purchase/Sale

Sale Consideration

Cost of Acquisition

Other relevant information

      

5. Deemed Capital Gains

Type of Asset

Type of Capital Gain (Short-Term/Long-Term)

Section under which the asset falls

Asset Transferred Year

New Asset Acquired Year

Amount Invested in CGAS (Capital Gain Account Scheme)

Amount Utilized

Deemed Capital Gain if the amount is not invested in CGAS

      

6. Slump Sale

Date of Transaction

Sale/Purchase Value

Exemption-related details

      

7. Others (e.g., Debt-Oriented Mutual Funds, Unquoted Shares, Foreign Exchange Assets, etc.)

Nature of Asset

Purchase and Sale Dates

Sale Consideration

Purchase Cost

Transfer Expenses

Cost and Date of Improvement (if applicable)

      

Ensure all details are accurately entered into the respective annexures to reflect correct computations in the ITR. This process will help you efficiently handle various capital gain scenarios for your clients.
