Follow these steps to accurately feed clubbing income in Express ITR:

Step 1: Access the ITR Summary

Open the client profile in Express ITR.

Navigate to the ITR Summary section.

Select Return Flow to proceed to the next step.

      

Step 2: Navigate to the Clubbing Income Section

Locate and click on the Clubbing Income head.

      

Step 3: Enter Clubbing Income Details

Under the Clubbing section, provide details of the income belonging to specified persons such as a spouse, minor child etc.

      
Input the following details:

Name

PAN

Aadhar

Relationship

Head of Income

Amount

Note: Ensure all information is accurate and complete to avoid discrepancies during return filing.

By following this process, you can efficiently feed clubbing income details into the system.
