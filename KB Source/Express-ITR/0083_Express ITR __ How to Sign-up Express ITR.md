How to Sign-up:

Step 1:

For Sign-Up  Click Here to Open

Step 2:

Fill in the required information and carefully select the organization/firm type After then Click on Sign Up Button:

Step 3:

You have received an OTP on your mentioned mobile number. Enter the OTP and click on the 'Verify' button.

After completing the verification, log in to the software using your ID (Mobile number) and password (created during sign-up)
