Follow these simple steps to export the Client Master from ExpressITR into an Excel sheet:

Step 1: Log in to ExpressITR

Open the ExpressITR software and log in with your credentials.
      

Step 2: Export Client Master

Once logged in, the Client Master screen will be displayed.
Click on the Excel icon in front of the Client Master option.
      
The Client Master Excel file will be automatically downloaded to your system.

By following these steps, you can seamlessly export your Client Master data in Excel format for easy access and management.
