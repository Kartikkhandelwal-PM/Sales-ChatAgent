# ExpressITR || Quantitive Details (Schedule QD)

To feed Quantitative Details (Schedule QD) in Express ITR, follow these steps:

Step 1: Access the ITR Summary

Open the client’s profile in the Express ITR application.

In the ITR Summary section, locate Return Flow and click to proceed.

      

Step 2: Go to Business and Profession Head

Open the Business Profession Head section.

      

Step 3: Access Schedule QD

In Business Information, click on Schedule QD to enter quantitative details.

      

If you are a Trading Concern then fill the Quantity Details in "Trading".

If You are a Manufacturing Concern then fill the Quantity Details in "Raw Material (Manufacturing) and Finished Products/ By-Products (Manufacturing)".

      

By following these steps, you can accurately enter quantitative details for different types of businesses in Express ITR.
