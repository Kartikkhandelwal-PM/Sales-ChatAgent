Specified Business Income Under Section 35AD in Express ITR

To record "Specified Business" income under Section 35AD in Express ITR, follow these steps:

Step 1: Access the Return Flow

Open the client profile in Express ITR.

In the ITR Summary section, select Return Flow to start.

      

Step 2: Go to Business and Profession Head

Navigate to the Business Profession Head section.

      

Step 3: Add Business Information

Click on Add Business and enter the following details:
Type

Business Name

Nature of Business

Trade Name

Description

      
Once all details are filled, click Done or press Esc to save.

Step 4: Access Income From Specified Activity Option

Click on Consolidate Sch. BP.

      
Scroll down to find the Specified Activity option.

      

Step 5: Complete Annexure Details

Click on Annexure and fill in all required details related to the Specified Business income.

      

By following these steps, you can accurately enter specified business income details for your client in Express ITR under Section 35AD.
