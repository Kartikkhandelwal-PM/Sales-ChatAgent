# ExpressITR || Steps to Download the Computation in PDF

Step 1: Access the ITR Summary

Begin by opening the client's profile in the Express ITR application.
In the client’s profile, locate and click on the ITR Summary section.
Within the ITR Summary, find the Return Flow option, then click on the corresponding button to proceed.

      
Step 2: Open Computation
From the list of options, select Computation to view the detailed tax computation for the client.

      
This will display a summary of the tax calculations and other related details for the selected ITR.

Step 3: Download the Computation as a PDF
Locate and click on the PDF ICON option.

      
Once selected, the system will automatically generate a PDF version of the computation. The file will be saved to your default download location on your system.

Following these steps will allow you to successfully download the Computation in PDF format.
