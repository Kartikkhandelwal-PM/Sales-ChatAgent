Steps to Download the Computation / Form 3CD in PDF with Different Font Sizes

Steps to Download the Computation in PDF

Step 1: Access the ITR Summary

Open the client's profile in the Express ITR application.

Locate and click on the ITR Summary section within the client’s profile.

In the ITR Summary section, find the Return Flow option and click the corresponding button to proceed.

      

Step 2: Open Computation

From the list of options, select Computation.

      
This will display the detailed tax computation for the client, including a summary of tax calculations and related details.

Step 3: Download the Computation as a PDF

Locate the Dropdown Button positioned before the PDF Icon.

Use the dropdown to select the desired font size.

      

After selecting the font size, click the PDF Icon. The system will automatically generate a PDF version of the computation.

The PDF file will be saved to your default download location on your system.

By following these steps, you can successfully download the Computation in PDF format with your preferred font size.

Steps to Download the 3CD in PDF

Step 1: Access the Audit Summary

Open the client's profile in the Express ITR application.

Locate and click on the Audit Summary section within the client’s profile.

In the Audit Summary section, find the Audit Status option and click the corresponding button to proceed.

      

Step 2: Open Form 3CB-3CD / Form 3CA-3CD

From the UI, select Form 3CB-3CD or Form 3CA-3CD, depending on the client’s status.

      
Within the same UI, select Form 3CD.

      

Step 3: Download the 3CD in PDF

Locate the Font Size Dropdown Button positioned before the PDF Icon.

      
Use the dropdown to select the desired font size.

After selecting the font size, click the PDF Icon. The system will automatically generate a PDF version of Form 3CD.

The PDF file will be saved to your default download location on your system.

By following these steps, you can successfully download the Form 3CD in PDF format with your preferred font size.
