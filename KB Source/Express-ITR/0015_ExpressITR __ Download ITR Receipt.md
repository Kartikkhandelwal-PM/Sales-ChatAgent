How to Download ITR Receipt in Express ITR

To download the ITR Receipt for a client in Express ITR, follow these steps:

Step 1: Log In and Open Return Register

Log in to the Express ITR application.

Navigate to the Client Master page, which will open automatically after login.

At the top of the page, locate and click on the Return Register option.

      

Step 2: Select the Assessment Year

Choose the relevant Assessment Year to proceed with the correct ITR records.

      

Step 3: Download the ITR Receipt

In the Return Register page, find the ITR Receipt option.

Use the search bar to locate the specific client.

      
Click on the PDF icon under ITR Receipt to start the download.

      
The ITR Receipt PDF will automatically download and open on your screen.

By following these steps, you can efficiently download the ITR Receipt for your client.
