This article provides step-by-step instructions to preview and download Schedule III in Express ITR. The Schedule III statement presents detailed financial information related to a client’s business or professional income and is an essential part of the ITR filing process.

Step 1: Open the ITR Summary

Open the required client profile from the dashboard.

Navigate to the ITR Summary section.

Click Return Flow to proceed to the return workflow.

      

Step 2: Navigate to Income Details

In the ITR Summary, select the Business Profession Income head.

This section displays income details pertaining to the client’s business or profession.

      

Step 3: Access the Schedule III

Within the Business Profession Income section, click on Schedule III.

The system will display the Schedule III statement associated with the selected client.

      

Step 4: Preview Schedule III

Click the Preview button to view Schedule III on-screen.

Review the displayed Schedule III data carefully before downloading.

      

Step 5: Download as PDF

To save a copy of the statement, click Download PDF.

The Schedule III document will be downloaded and saved in PDF format for your records, review, or submission.

      

Notes:

Ensure that all Schedule III data has been reviewed and finalised before downloading the Schedule III statement.

The downloaded PDF can be used for record-keeping, client sharing, or audit reference.
