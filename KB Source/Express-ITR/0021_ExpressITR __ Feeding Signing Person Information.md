Feeding Signing Person Information

To enter the signing person’s details in Express ITR, please follow these steps:

Open ITR Summary:

Access the client profile in Express ITR.

In the ITR Summary section, select Return Flow to continue.

      

Proceed to E-Filing Options:

Navigate to the E-Filing section and click on Review and E-Filing Options.

      

      

Input Signing Person Information:

On the E-Filing page, enter the required information for the signing person like Capacity, Signing Person Details.
      

Following these steps will ensure accurate completion of the signing person’s details.
