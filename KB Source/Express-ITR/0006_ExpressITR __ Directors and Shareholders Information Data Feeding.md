Directors and Shareholders Information in ExpressITR

For Individuals or Firms

Step 1: Access Client Return Flow

Open the client profile and go to the ITR Summary.

Click on the Return Flow option.

      

Step 2: Navigate to E-Filing

Select the E-Filing option.

      
Step 3: Enter Directors/Shareholding Details

Open Schedule DIR/Shareholding Details.

      
Fill in the relevant information for directors and shareholders.

For Companies

Step 1: Open Business and Profession Head

Navigate to the Business Profession section.

      

Step 2: Select Business Information

Click on Business Information and proceed to Origination Info.

      

Step 3: DPIIT Information & E-Filing

Select DPIIT information as required, then click on the E-Filing option.

      

Step 4: Enter Shareholding Information

Go to Sch Shareholding Option and enter the relevant shareholder details.

      

This guide provides clear steps for entering Directors and Shareholders information for both individuals/firms and companies in ExpressITR.
