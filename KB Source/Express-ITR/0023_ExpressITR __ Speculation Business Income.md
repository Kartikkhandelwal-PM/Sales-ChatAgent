Speculation Business Income in Express ITR

To record "Speculation Business" income for a client in Express ITR, follow these steps:

Step 1: Access the Return Flow

Open the client’s profile in Express ITR.

In the ITR Summary section, select Return Flow to begin.

      

Step 2: Go to Business Profession Head

Navigate to the Business Profession Head section.

      

Step 3: Add Business Information

Click on Add Business and enter the following details: 
Type

Business Name

Nature of Business

Trade Name

Description

      
Once entered, click Done or press Esc to save the business details.

Step 4: Access Income From Speculation Activity Option
Click on Consolidate Sch. BP.

      
Scroll down and locate the Speculation Activity option.

      

Step 5: Complete Annexure Details

Click on Annexure and fill in all required details related to the Speculation Activity income.

      

By completing these steps, you can accurately enter "Speculation Business" income details for your client in Express ITR.
