Financial Statements Data Entry Process

Step 1: Access the Return Flow

Log in to the client’s profile in Express ITR.

Navigate to the ITR Summary section and select Return Flow to initiate the process.

      

Step 2: Navigate to the Business Profession Head

Open the Business Profession Head section to proceed.

      

Step 3: Enter Business Information

Click Add Business and provide the following details:

Type of Business

Business Name

Nature of Business

Trade Name

Description

      
After entering the details, click Done or press Esc to save the information.

Step 4: Populate Financial Statements

Select Financial Statements and input the required annexures under Financial Statements Notes, such as:

Capital Account

Sundry Debtors

Sundry Creditors

Investments

Inventory

Short-Term Investments

Bad Debts

      

Step 5: Add Income Details

Open the specific account where data needs to be entered.

Click the dropdown arrow and select the appropriate option.

Use the (+) button to input income details.

      

      

Additional Notes: For other financial statements, such as the Manufacturing Account and Trading Account, follow the same steps outlined above.
