# ExpressITR || Import GST Turnover Details From GST Portal

**Section:** Process

**Category:** Express-ITR

**Source:** https://support.kdksoftware.com/portal/en/kb/articles/import-gst-turnover-details-from-gst-portal

**Tags:** schedule gst, import gst turnover details, import gst, import gst turnover, import gst turnover details from gst portal, gst turnover details, expressitr, gst turnover

---

To import GST Turnover Details from the GST Portal into Express ITR, follow these steps:

Step 1: Access the ITR Summary

Open the client profile in Express ITR.

In the ITR Summary section, click on Return Flow to proceed.

      

Step 2: Go to Business and Profession Head

Open the Business Profession Head section.

      

Step 3: Access GST Details Option

In the Business Information area, click on GST Details.

      

Step 4: Input GST Credentials and Import Turnover Details

Enter the GSTN, GSTN Username, and GSTN Password.

Click on Get Captcha to retrieve a captcha code.

Enter the captcha code, then click Verify.

After verification, your GST Turnover details will be imported from the GST Portal.

      

      

Following these steps will help you import your client’s GST turnover details accurately into Express ITR.
