Process to Import 3CD Data in Business Profession Head

Follow the steps below to import 3CD data into the Business Profession Head:

Step 1: Access Client Profile

Log in to Express ITR and open the profile of the client whose audit details need to be updated.

Navigate to the ITR Summary section.

Click on Return Flow to access the relevant ITR options.

      

Step 2: Navigate to Business Profession Head

Locate the Business Profession Head section in the menu.

      
Click to open it.

Step 3: Add Business Information

Click on Add Business and fill in the required details:

Type: Specify the type of business.

Business Name: Enter the name of the business.

Nature of Business: Choose the nature of the business from the available options.

Trade Name: Provide the trade name, if applicable.

Description: Enter a brief description of the business.

                  

Step 4: Import 3CD Data

Navigate to the Consolidated Schedule BP section.

Click on the Import From 3CD option.

      

After completing this process, the 3CD data will be successfully imported into the Business Head, including: Depreciation Chart, Disallowances, Amounts not credited to the Profit and Loss Account, and other relevant details.

By following these steps, you can efficiently import 3CD data into the Business Profession Head.
