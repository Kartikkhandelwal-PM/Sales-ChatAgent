Salary Income Entry Process in Express ITR

To enter Salary Income in Express ITR, follow these steps:

Step 1: Access ITR Summary

Open the client profile within Express ITR.

In the ITR Summary section, select Return Flow to proceed.

      

Step 2: Open Salary Income Head

Navigate to the Salary Income Head section to enter the salary information.

      

Step 3: Input Employer Details
Select the Employer Type (e.g., Government, PSU, Private, etc.).

      
Enter the employer’s details, including:

Name

TAN

Address

      

Step 4: Enter Gross Salary Details

Under Gross Salary, click the + button to enter detailed income information.

Specify components such as Basic Salary, Allowances and Perquisites received from the employer.

      

Step 5: Enter Exemptions and Deductions

Under Overall Exemptions & Deductions, click the + button to input relevant exemptions:Exemptions against Allowances and Perquisites

Other eligible exemptions.

      

Following this process ensures accurate and comprehensive entry of salary income for clients in Express ITR.
