Download Filed ITR Form in Express ITR

To download the filed ITR form using Express ITR, follow these steps:

Step 1: Access the ITR Summary

Open the client’s profile in the Express ITR application.

Within the client profile, navigate to the ITR Summary section.

      
Step 2: Download the Filed ITR Form

In the ITR Summary, locate the ITR Form option, where the previously filed ITR form type will be displayed.

Click on the specific ITR form shown to initiate the download.

      
The ITR form will automatically download and open on your screen.

By following these steps, you can quickly and efficiently download the filed ITR form for your client.
