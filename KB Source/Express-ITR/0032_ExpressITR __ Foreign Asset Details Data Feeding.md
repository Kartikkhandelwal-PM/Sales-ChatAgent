Foreign Asset Details Data Feeding in Express ITR

To record Foreign Asset Details in Express ITR, follow these steps:

Step 1: Access the ITR Summary

Open the client profile in Express ITR.
In the ITR Summary section, click on Return Flow to proceed.

      

Step 2: Navigate to the E-Filing Option

Click on the E-Filing option.

      

Step 3: Enter Foreign Asset Details in Schedule FA

Select Schedule FA and enter the relevant foreign asset details.

      

      

By following these steps, you can accurately record your client’s foreign asset details in Express ITR.
