Exempt Income Data Feeding in Express ITR

To record Exempt Income in Express ITR, follow these steps:

Step 1: Access the Return Flow

Open the client profile in Express ITR.

In the ITR Summary section, click on Return Flow to proceed.

      

Step 2: Navigate to Exempt Income Head

Go to the Exempt Income Head section.

      

      

Step 3: Enter Exempt Income Details

      
Click on the + button to add details.

      

      
Enter all relevant exempt income information as needed.

By following these steps, you can efficiently enter exempt income for your client in Express ITR.
