ERROR : Nature of Business (Other than declared u/s 44AD, 44ADA,44AE) is missing. Please enter.

Step 1: Click on the Error Message

While generating the JSON file, if you encounter this error, click on the error message link.

      
Step 2: Redirect to Consolidated Schedule BP

The software will take you to the Consolidated Schedule BP section.

Step 3: Check Business Income Details

Verify if you have declared business income other than presumptive income under Sections 44AD, 44ADA, or 44AE.

Ensure that the Business Type and Nature of Business fields are correctly selected and correspond to the declared income.
Step 4: Create or Update Business Entry

If there is no business entry, create a new one for ordinary business income.

      
Select the appropriate Nature of Business from the provided list.

      

Step 5: Save the Changes

Click on "Done" or press the Esc key to save the updated information.

Step 6: Regenerate the JSON File

Go back to the main menu or dashboard.

Generate the JSON file again. The error should now be resolved.

Following these steps will ensure the correct entry of information and resolve the issue.

Nature of Business u/s 44AE is missing. Please enter.

If you encounter this error while generating the JSON file, follow these steps to resolve it:

      
Step 1: Locate the Error

Click on the error message link to be redirected to the Consolidated Schedule BP.
Step 2: Verify Income Details

Check if you have declared business income under presumptive taxation sections (44AD, 44ADA, or 44AE).

Ensure the Nature of Business field is not left blank according to these entries.

Step 3: Create or Update Business Entry

If a presumptive income entry is missing, create a new business entry under the relevant section.

      
Select the appropriate Nature of Business from the available options.

      

Step 4: Save the Information

Click on "Done" or press the Esc key to save the updated details.

Step 5: Regenerate the JSON File

Return to the main dashboard and generate the JSON file again.

The error should now be resolved.

Following these steps will ensure the correct entry of information and resolve the issue.
