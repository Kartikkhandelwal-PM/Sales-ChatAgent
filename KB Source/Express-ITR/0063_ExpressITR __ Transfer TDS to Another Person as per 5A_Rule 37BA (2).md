How to Transfer TDS to Another Person’s as per 5A/Rule 37BA(2) in Express ITR
​

This process enables you to transfer your TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) to another person's as per 5A/Rule 37BA(2). Please follow these steps:

Step 1: Access the ITR Summary

Open the client profile in Express ITR.

In the ITR Summary section, click on Return Flow to proceed.

      

Step 2: Navigate to Tax Details

Go to the Tax Details section.

      

Step 3: Update TDS Entry

First, update your TDS entry.

Then, remove the TDS amount from the Claimed in Own Hands column.

      

Step 4: Open 5A/Rule 37BA(2) Annexure

Access the 5A/Rule 37BA(2) Details annexure.

      

Step 5: Enter Recipient’s Details

Enter the details of the person to whom you want to transfer the TDS.

      

To Claim Other Person TDS Amount as per Rule 5A/37BA (2), please follow the knowledgebase provided → Follow Me !

Upon
 completion of these steps, the TDS will be forwarded to the other 
person’s account, allowing it to be claimed under their profile.
