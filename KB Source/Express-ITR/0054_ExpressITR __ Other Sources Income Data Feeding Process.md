Other Sources Income Data Feeding Process

To record income from other sources in Express ITR, follow the steps outlined below:

Step 1: Access the ITR Summary

Open the client profile in Express ITR.

In the ITR Summary section, select Return Flow to continue.

      

Step 2: Navigate to Other Sources Income

Proceed to the Other Sources Income head.

      

Step 3: Enter Income Details Under "Gross Income Chargeable at Normal Rate"

Enter the relevant Income types, including:

Dividend Income under Section 56(2)(i)

Saving Bank Interest

Fixed Deposit (FDR) Interest

Interest on Income Tax Refund

Gift Income under Section 56(2)(x)

Family Pension

Rent from Plant & Machinery or Furniture

Other Interest

Interest on Compensation

      

Expenses Section

Record any related expenses.

      
Depreciation

Record any related Depreciation.

      

Additional Categories

Gross Income Chargeable at Special Rate: Input any income subject to special tax rates.

      
Amount Not Deductible under Section 58

Profit Chargeable to Tax under Section 59

      

Following these steps will help ensure that you accurately record data for income from other sources in Express ITR.
