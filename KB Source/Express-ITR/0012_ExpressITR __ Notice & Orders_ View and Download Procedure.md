# ExpressITR || Notice & Orders: View and Download Procedure

**Category:** Express-ITR

You can easily view, download, and track your Notices and Orders in ExpressITR by following the steps below:

Step 1: Access the Notices & Orders Section

Go to the “Notice Orders” option available in the main menu.

      

Step 2: Select the Assessment Year

Choose the relevant Assessment Year from the dropdown list or available options.

      

Step 3: View Available Notices and Orders

After selecting the Assessment Year, the corresponding Notices and Orders will be displayed on your screen.

      

Step 4: View Notice Details

To open a specific document, click on “View Details” under the Action column.

      

Step 5: Download the PDF

In the details window, click on “Download PDF” to save the notice or order to your system.

      

Step 6: Use the Search Bar

Quickly find a record using the Search Bar by entering a Notice ID, Client Name, or PAN.

      

Step 7: Check Notice Status

To view the current status of a notice:

Select Pending, Closed, or Responded to see relevant details.

      

Step 8: View Due Dates

Click on the “Due Date” option.

Select a due date to display all related details and upcoming actions.
      

Step 9: View Assessment Year-Wise Details

To check details for a specific year, click on the “Assessment Year” option, choose the required year, and the corresponding records will appear.

      

Summary: With ExpressITR, you can view, download, and manage all Notices & Orders in one place. The dashboard also allows you to search, track status, and monitor due dates with ease and accuracy.
