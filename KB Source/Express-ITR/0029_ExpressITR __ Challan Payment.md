The following steps outline the process for making an online challan payment through Express ITR.

Step 1: Navigate to the Challan Payment Section

From the Dashboard, select the Pay Challan option to initiate the challan payment process.

      

Step 2: Enter Assessment Year and Select Mode of Payment

In the Challan Payment window:

Specify the applicable Assessment Year, and

Choose the desired Mode of Payment from the available options.

      

Step 3: Select the Type of Payment (Minor Head)

After selecting the mode of payment, choose the relevant Type of Payment (Minor Head) based on the nature of the transaction.

      

Step 4: Confirm and Proceed to Payment

Once all mandatory fields have been completed, click on Pay Now to proceed.

      

Step 5: Review Details on the ITD Portal

The entered details will be automatically populated on the Income Tax Department (ITD) portal.

Verify all information displayed, and then click Pay Now on the portal to continue.

      
      

Step 6: Complete the Payment Transaction

Enter the required payment credentials or details as per the selected payment mode and finalize the transaction.

By following the above steps, you can seamlessly complete your Challan Payment through Express ITR — all from a single, integrated dashboard in just a few clicks.
