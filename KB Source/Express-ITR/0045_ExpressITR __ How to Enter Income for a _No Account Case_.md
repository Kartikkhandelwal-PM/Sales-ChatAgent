How to Enter Income for a "No Account Case" in Express ITR

To input income under a "No Account Case" in Express ITR, follow these detailed steps:

Step 1: Access Return Flow

Open the client's profile in Express ITR.

In the ITR Summary section, select Return Flow to begin.

      

Step 2: Go to Business and Profession Head

Navigate to the Business Profession Head section.

      

Step 3: Add Business Information

Click on Add Business and enter relevant details such as: 

Type, 

Business Name, 

Nature of Business, 

Trade Name, 

Description

      
Once the information is entered, click Done or press Esc to save the business details.

Step 4: Access No Account Case Option

Click on "Consolidate Sch. BP" Option.

      
Scroll down to locate the "No Accounts Case" option.

      

Step 5: Complete Annexure Details

Click on Annexure and fill in all necessary details related to the "No Account Case" income.

      

By following these steps, you can efficiently enter "No Account Case" income details for your client in Express ITR.
