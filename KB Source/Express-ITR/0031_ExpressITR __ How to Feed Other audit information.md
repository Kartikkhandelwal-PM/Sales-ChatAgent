Other Audit Report Information in Express ITR

To add Other Audit Report Details in Express ITR, follow these organized steps to ensure accurate entry:

Step 1: Access the Return Flow

Open the client’s profile in Express ITR.

In the ITR Summary section, select Return Flow to proceed.

      

Step 2: Navigate to the Business and Profession Head

Within the Return Flow menu, go to the Business Profession Head section.

      

Step 3: Enter Auditor Information

Open the Auditor Information section.

      
Respond to the prompt: "If liable to furnish other audit report under the Income-tax Act/Other Act, mention whether have you furnished such report. If yes, please provide the details as under"

Select Yes if applicable.

      
Provide the required audit details.
Use the + button to add new rows for additional entries, if needed.

      

By following these steps, you can accurately record other audit report information in Express ITR, maintaining compliance with reporting standards.
