Steps to Export Capital Gain Data in Excel
Step 1: Access the ITR Summary
Open the client’s profile in the Express ITR application.
Navigate to the ITR Summary section within the client profile.
Click the button under Return Flow to view additional options.

      

Step 2: Select the Capital Gain Section
Click on the Capital Gain heading to access the capital gain data.

      

Step 3: Export the Capital Gain Data
Click on the Export CG Data option.

      
This will download the capital gain data as an Excel file to your system.

Your capital gain data has now been successfully downloaded in Excel format.
