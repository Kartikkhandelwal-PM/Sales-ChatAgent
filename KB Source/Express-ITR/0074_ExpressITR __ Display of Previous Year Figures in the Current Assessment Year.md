Purpose 

This article explains how Previous Year (PY) figures are automatically displayed within the Current Assessment Year (AY) ITR form in the software. This functionality helps users compare year-on-year data and ensure accurate return preparation.

Feature Overview

The software automatically fetches and displays Previous Year figures when the user opens the Return Flow for the selected Assessment Year. These figures appear across relevant income heads, deductions, and tax liability sections for reference.

This automatic display enables quick comparison and verification without requiring manual entry.

Steps to View Previous Year Figures

Step 1: Open the Client Profile

Select and open the required client profile from the dashboard.

Step 2: Navigate to the Current Assessment Year ITR Form

Open the ITR form for the relevant current Assessment Year.

Step 3: Access Income or Deduction Heads

Navigate to any income head such as:

Salary

House Property

Business/Profession

Capital Gains

Other Sources

      
The system will automatically display the Previous Year figures related to that head.

Where Previous Year Figures Are Displayed

Previous Year data is automatically shown across the following sections:

All Income Heads

Deductions (e.g., Chapter VI-A)

Tax Liability tab

Financial Statements (if applicable)

This ensures consistency, transparency, and ease of review during return preparation.

Benefits of This Feature

Eliminates manual data entry of previous year values

Provides instant year-on-year data comparison

Helps identify unusual variances and ensure accuracy

Improves workflow efficiency during ITR preparation
