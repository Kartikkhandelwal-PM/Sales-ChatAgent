Asset and Liabilities Information (Schedule AL) in ExpressITR

To accurately enter Schedule AL data, follow these steps:

Step 1: Access the Client Return Flow

Open the client profile and navigate to the Return Summary.

Click on the Return Flow option to proceed.

      

Step 2: Navigate to e-Filing

Select the e-Filing option to access the filing section.

      

Step 3: Enter Schedule AL Information

Under the Basic Information section, click on Schedule AL.

Fill in the relevant details pertaining to assets and liabilities.

      

This guide provides a straightforward process for entering Asset and Liabilities information (Schedule AL) in ExpressITR, ensuring accuracy and clarity throughout.
