How to View and Work on "Returns in Progress" in ExpressTDS

Follow these steps to manage and resume TDS returns in progress:

Step 1: Navigate to Deductor Master

Log in to ExpressTDS.

Go to the Deductor Master section to access TDS returns and company details.

Step 2: Select the Company

From the list of available companies/deductors, select the relevant company for which you need to download or manage TDS data.
Step 3: Access Returns in Progress

In the Deductor's Dashboard, locate and select the Returns in Progress option.

      
This will display a list of TDS returns that are currently in progress.

Step 4: Resume Preparation

Identify the specific return (Regular or Correction) you want to work on.

      
Click on the return to resume its preparation:
For Regular Returns: Continue adding or reviewing income and TDS data.

For Correction Returns: Edit and rectify the required details.

Tips:

Save Regularly: Always save your work to avoid data loss.

Monitor Deadlines: Ensure returns in progress are filed within the due date.

Check for Errors: Use the validation feature to identify and resolve errors before finalizing.

By using the "Returns in Progress" feature, you can efficiently manage and complete TDS returns for your clients.
