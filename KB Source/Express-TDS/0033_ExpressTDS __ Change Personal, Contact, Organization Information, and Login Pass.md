How to Change Personal, Contact, Organization Information, and Login Password in ExpressTDS

To keep your personal, contact, and organization information, as well as your login password up-to-date in ExpressTDS, follow these steps:

1. Access Profile Settings

Log in to ExpressTDS and click on the Profile Icon located in the top right corner.

      
From the dropdown menu, select Profile Settings to access your account settings.

      

2. Update Personal & Contact Information

In Profile Settings, go to the Personal & Contact Information section.

Click the pencil icon to edit details, including the contact person’s name, mobile number, and email address.

      
After entering the new information, click Save to apply the changes.

3. Change Organization Information

Navigate to the Organization Information section to update the organization name as needed.

      
Once updated, click Save to confirm changes.

Note: Organization/Firm type cannot be changed.

4. Change Login Password

To update your login password, select Reset Password in the profile settings.

      
Enter the new password as prompted and save it to complete the change.

Following these steps will help ensure that your profile and account information in ExpressTDS remains accurate and current.
