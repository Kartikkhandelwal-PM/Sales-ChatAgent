TDS Rate Finder

Step 1: Open your web browser and navigate to https://www.expresstds.com/
Step 2: Select the Rate Finder option from the menu.

      

Step 3: Fill in the required details using the dropdown menus:Financial Year: If the transaction date is on or after 01/10/2024, ensure the checkbox is selected.

      
Type of Deductor

      
Deductee Details

      
Section Details

      

Step 4: Click on the Rate Icon to view the applicable TDS rates based on the selected inputs.

      

      

Using this process, you can efficiently find the TDS section rates.
