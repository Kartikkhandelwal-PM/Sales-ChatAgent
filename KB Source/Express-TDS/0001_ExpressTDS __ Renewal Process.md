Renewing your ExpressTDS subscription is a seamless process designed to ensure uninterrupted access to the platform’s comprehensive features and services. Follow these detailed steps to complete your subscription renewal effortlessly.

      

Steps to Renew Your ExpressTDS Subscription

Step 1: Access Profile Settings

To begin, log in to your ExpressTDS account and navigate to the top-right corner of the screen. Click on the Profile Settings option to access your account settings.
Step 2: Open the Subscription Plan Section

Within your profile, locate and click on the Subscription Plan tab. This section provides an overview of your current plan and renewal options.

      
Step 3: Initiate the Renewal Process

Click on the Renew Now button to proceed. This will guide you to the plan selection page.

      

Step 4: Select a Subscription Plan

Browse the list of available plans and choose the one that best aligns with your requirements. Once you’ve made your selection, click the Select button to confirm.

      

Step 5: Enter Billing Information

Provide accurate billing details in the designated fields. Once all required information has been entered, click on the Make Payment button to process the transaction securely.

      

Step 6: Confirm Payment

Upon successful payment, your subscription will be renewed instantly. A confirmation email or notification will be sent to your registered account.

Step 7: Re-login to Finalize Changes

For the renewal to take full effect, log out and log back into your ExpressTDS account. This step ensures all updates are synchronized with your account.

By following these steps, you can easily renew your ExpressTDS subscription and maintain uninterrupted access to its robust features and services.
