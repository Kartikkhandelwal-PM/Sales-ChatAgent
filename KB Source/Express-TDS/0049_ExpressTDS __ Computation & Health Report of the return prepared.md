Purpose of Health & Computation Checks

The health and computation checks are essential for ensuring the accuracy and completeness of the prepared return. This step is crucial for:

Verifying Reporting: Allows for a detailed review of the data reported in the return.

Error Prevention: Identifies potential errors or discrepancies, helping to avoid warnings or issues during the File Validation Utility (FVU) generation and filing process.

Ensuring Compliance: Confirms that the return meets all regulatory requirements.

These checks are vital for a smooth filing experience and maintaining compliance with tax regulations.

Step-by-Step Process for Health & Computation Checks

Step 1: Navigate to Deductor Master

Select the relevant company or deductor from the list.

Go to Return Summary and choose the created return.

Step 2: View the Created Return

Click View for the selected return.

Step 3: Select Computation & Health Option

Locate and click on the Computation & Health option to access health and computation checks.

      

Step 4: Download Transactions

Click on the Download option to view and review the details in Excel format.

      

This allows you to Download the Computation & Health report for a detailed assessment in Excel Format.
