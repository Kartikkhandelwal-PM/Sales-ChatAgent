# ExpressTDS || Salary TDS Calculator

**Section:** Express TDS

**Category:** Express-TDS

**Source:** https://support.kdksoftware.com/portal/en/kb/articles/how-to-view-tds-calculator-report

**Tags:** diy plan, expresstds, salary tds calculator

---

The Salary TDS Calculator in Express TDS is a tool designed to assist users in accurately calculating TDS based on income, payments, or transactions subject to tax deduction. It automates the calculation process, ensuring compliance with the latest tax laws and minimizing human error.

Steps to View the Salary TDS Calculator Report:

Step 1: Access the Express TDS portal by entering your username and password. Once logged in, you will be directed to the Deductor Master page, where all deductors are listed.
Step 2: Select the desired deductor from the list to proceed. This will bring you to a page where the Salary TDS Calculator section is visible.
Step 3: Click on "Salary TDS Calc" to open the Salary TDS Calculator.

      

Step 4: An Import Excel File wizard will appear. You can either:
Import a pre-filled Salary TDS Excel file, or

Download an Excel template to fill in and upload later.

      

Step 5: If you don’t have the template, click Download Excel Template, and fill in the relevant salary and TDS data.

      

Step 6: Once the Excel file is ready, click Browse to locate and select the file from your computer. Select the Salary TDS calculator Excel file and click Open.

      

Step 7: After selecting the file, click Submit to import the Salary TDS Calculator Excel file into Express TDS.
Step 8: Upon successful import, the Employee-Wise Salary Details section will open, displaying the salary data for each employee.

      

Step 9: To view the detailed TDS computation report, click View.

      

Step 10: To see the monthly breakdown of TDS for each employee, click Month Wise.

By following these steps, you can successfully view the Salary TDS Calculator report in Express TDS.
