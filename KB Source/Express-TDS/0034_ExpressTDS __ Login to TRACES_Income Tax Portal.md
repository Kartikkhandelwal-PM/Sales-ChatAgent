Process to Login at TRACES/Income Tax Portal through Express TDS

Here is a step-by-step guide to access the TRACES and Income Tax portals through Express TDS:

Step 1: Login to Express TDS

Open a web browser and go to https://www.expresstds.com/app/login.

Enter your User ID & Password to log in.

Step 2: Select Company/Deductor

After logging in, locate the section to select the Company/Deductor.

      
Choose the correct organization or entity for which you need TDS-related services, especially if you manage multiple entities.

Step 3: Login to the TRACES Portal

After selecting the company/deductor, look for the TRACES icon.

      
Click on this icon, and it will redirect you to the TRACES portal login page.

Step 4: Login to the Income Tax Portal

In the same dashboard, find the ITD icon.

      
Click on the ITD icon to be redirected to the Income Tax Portal (https://eportal.incometax.gov.in/iec/foservices/#/login).

Important Tips:

Update User ID and Password: Ensure that the credentials for both TRACES and Income Tax portals are updated and accurate.

Reset Forgotten Password: If you’ve forgotten your password, use the password reset option on the respective portal’s login page.

By following these steps, you can smoothly access TRACES and the Income Tax portal through Express TDS.
