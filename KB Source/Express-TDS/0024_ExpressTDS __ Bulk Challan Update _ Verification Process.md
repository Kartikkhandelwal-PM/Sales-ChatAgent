Bulk Challan Update / Verification Process in ExpressTDS
Follow the step-by-step guide below to efficiently update and verify challans in ExpressTDS.

Step 1: Log in to the Website

Open your web browser and go to https://www.expresstds.com/
Enter your username and password, then click Login to access your account.

Step 2: Access the Deductor Master

Once logged in, navigate to the Deductor Dashboard.
Select the relevant company or deductor from the list.

      

Step 3: Update Challan

Within the Deductor Master, click on the Update Challan option.

      
After the synchronization process is complete, the challan status will be updated from the portal.

Step 4: View Updated Challan Status

Check the Challans Overview section to view the updated challan status.

      
Alternatively, you can view the updated challan status in the Challan Section of the created TDS return.

      

Important Notes:

Ensure that the correct Income Tax and TRACES credentials are entered for accurate challan status updates.

Once updated, users can download the pre-filled template in Excel format to obtain the default challan details.
