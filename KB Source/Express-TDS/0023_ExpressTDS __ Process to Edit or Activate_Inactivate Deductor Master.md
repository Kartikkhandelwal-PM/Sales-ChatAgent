Process to Edit or Activate/Inactivate Deductor Master in ExpressTDS

This guide outlines the step-by-step process to edit the Deductor Master and activate/inactivate a Deductor in ExpressTDS.

Steps to Edit Deductor Master

Step 1: Log in to the Website

Open your web browser and navigate to ExpressTDS Website.

Enter your username and password.

Click on Login to access your account.

      

Step 2: Access the Deductor Master

From the dashboard, click on the Deductor Master tab.

Locate the desired Deductor and click on the Action icon.

      

Step 3: Edit and Save

Update the necessary details for the Deductor.

      
Once changes are made, click on Save.

Steps to Activate/Inactivate a Deductor

Step 1: Select the Deductor

Navigate to the Deductor Master tab on the dashboard.

Click on the Action icon for the specific Deductor.

      

Step 2: Activate/Inactivate

Click on the Activate or Inactivate icon, depending on your requirement.

      

      
Confirm and click Save.

Using these steps, you can efficiently update Deductor details or modify their activation status in ExpressTDS. This ensures seamless management of your Deductor Master records.
