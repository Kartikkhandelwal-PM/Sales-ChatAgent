Step 1: Log in to ExpressTDS

Log in to the ExpressTDS portal using your username and password.
Once logged in, the Deductor Master page will open, displaying a list of all deductors.
Step 2: Select the Deductor
From the Deductor Master page, click on the desired deductor to access their details.

      

Step 3: Click on the Challan Overview Option
Navigate to the Challan Overview option and click on it.

      

Step 4: View Verified Unconsumed Challans
All verified TRACES unconsumed challans along with their mapped transactions will be displayed here.

      

Step 5: View Unconsumed Challans in the Created Return
Click on the View option for a created return to check the associated unconsumed challans.

      

Step 6: Access the Challan Option
Select the Challan option to proceed further.

      

Step 7: View Unconsumed Challans
In this section, you can view all unconsumed challans.

      
By following this process, you can easily view and utilize unconsumed challans based on your requirements.
