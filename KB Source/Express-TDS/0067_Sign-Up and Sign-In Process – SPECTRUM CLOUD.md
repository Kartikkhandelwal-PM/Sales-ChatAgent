This article provides a step-by-step guide to help you create an account and log in to the SPECTRUM CLOUD platform by KDK Software.

SPECTRUM CLOUD enables tax professionals to manage GST, Income Tax (ITR), and TDS compliance for all their clients from a single, secure cloud-based platform.

Note: If you are signing up as a business entity (e.g., SME, Corporate, Enterprise), please refer to the Business Users section below for the appropriate platforms.

🎯 Intended Users : The SPECTRUM CLOUD portal is designed for:

Chartered Accountants (CAs)
Accountants
Tax Practitioners
GST Practitioners (GSTPs)

📝 Step 1: Account Registration (Sign-Up)

To access the platform, you must first register an account.

Navigate to the Sign-Up page: https://app.kdksoftware.com/signup

Complete the registration form with the following:
Full Name
Email Address
Mobile Number
Other required details

Click "SIGN UP" to submit the form.

You will receive a One-Time Password (OTP) on your registered mobile number.

Enter the OTP in the designated field to verify your account.

🔐 Step 2: User Login (Sign-In)

Once your account is verified, follow the steps below to log in.

Go to the Login page: https://app.kdksoftware.com/login

Enter your registered mobile number and password.
Click "Login" to access your dashboard.

🏢 For Business Users (SMEs, Corporates, Enterprises)

If you are representing a Small or Medium Enterprise (SME), Corporate, or Enterprise, please use the appropriate platform designed specifically for business entities. These platforms provide tailored solutions to meet business compliance and filing requirements:

🔗 Recommended Platforms for Business Users:

ExpressGST – For GST filing and compliance 👉 https://www.expressgst.com/

ExpressITR – For Income Tax Return (ITR) filing 👉 https://www.expressitr.com/

ExpressTDS – For TDS return filing and related compliance 👉 https://www.expresstds.com/

Note: These platforms are optimized for organizations and business entities rather than individual professionals such as CAs or tax consultants.

⚠️ Important Guidelines

Password Confidentiality: Use a secure password and do not share your login credentials.
Consistent Credentials Across Products: If you use multiple KDK Software products with the same mobile number, please ensure you use the same password across platforms to maintain account consistency.

If you experience any issues during registration or login, please contact our customer support team.
