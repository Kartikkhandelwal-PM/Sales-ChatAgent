# ExpressTDS || Financial Year Selection/Change Process

**Section:** Express TDS

---

A financial year is a twelve-month period used by governments, businesses, and organizations for budgeting, financial reporting and tax calculations. 

In India, the financial year begins on April 1 and ends on March 31 of the following year.

To Select/Change the financial year in the ExpressTDS application, follow these steps:
Launch the ExpressTDS application and select the Deductor you wish to work with.

Navigate to the top-right corner of the interface, where the Financial Year dropdown menu is located.

From the dropdown menu, select the appropriate financial year as per your requirements.

Proceed with your tasks using the selected financial year.

This guide outlines the process for selecting or changing the financial year in ExpressTDS.
