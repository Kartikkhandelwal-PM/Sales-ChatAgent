Data Sync Process

Syncing TDS Returns, Acknowledgments, Challans, PAN Status, Notices and Orders and Raised Request Status data in the ExpressTDS software ensures your records remain accurate and up-to-date. 

Follow these steps to complete the data synchronization process:

Steps for Data Sync Using ExpressTDS Software

Step 1: Select the Client

Navigate to the Deductor Master section in the software.

Select the Deductor or company for whom you wish to sync data.

      

Step 2: Initiate Sync

Locate the Sync Option: Find the "Sync" button at the top-right corner of the page.

Click Sync: Start the synchronization process by clicking the "Sync" button.

      
Enter Captcha: Complete the CAPTCHA verification to confirm you are not a robot.

Proceed: After entering the CAPTCHA, click the "Proceed" button to continue.

      

💡 Important Notes

✔ Stable Internet Connection: Ensure a reliable connection to prevent sync interruptions.

✔ Updated Credentials: Verify that your TRACES, Income Tax, or Insight credentials are up to date.
📌 Ensure the latest Express Connector is running before syncing data.

By following these steps, your data will always stay synchronized and up-to-date with the portal.
