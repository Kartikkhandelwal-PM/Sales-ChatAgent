Deductor Master Download Process

Step 1: Log in to the ExpressTDS website at www.expresstds.com. Upon successful login, you will be automatically redirected to the Deductor Master page.

Step 2: Click on the Download icon to export the Deductor Master in Excel format.

      

Once downloaded, you can open the file in Excel to view a comprehensive list of deductor details for easy reference and verification.
