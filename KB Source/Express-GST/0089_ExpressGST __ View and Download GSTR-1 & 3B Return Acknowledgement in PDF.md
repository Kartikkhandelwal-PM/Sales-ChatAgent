Steps to Download Return Acknowledgement PDF :

Step 1: Access the Express GST portal by entering your username and password. After logging in, the Client Master page will appear, showing a list of all your clients.

      

Step 2: Select the client for whom you wish to download the filed return acknowledgment PDF. Click on the PDF icon for the relevant period to download the acknowledgment.

      

Step 3: Once you click on the PDF icon, the application will download the GSTR-1 or GSTR-3B acknowledgment PDF file for the selected period.

      

By following these steps, you can easily download the Return Acknowledgement PDF in Express GST.
