Viewing and Updating GSTN-ID Password Details in ExpressGST

To view, change, or update GSTN-ID password details in ExpressGST, follow these steps:

Step 1: Select the Company

Navigate to the company list and select the relevant company for which you want to manage GSTN-ID details.

Click on the Pencil icon next to the company name to access its settings.

      

Step 2: View or Update Credentials

Once in the settings, you can view the GSTN-ID credentials for the selected company.

From this screen, you can also change or update the password if needed.

Additionally, company details can be reviewed here for any necessary updates.

      

This process allows for quick access and updates to GSTN-ID credentials, ensuring accurate and up-to-date information in ExpressGST.
