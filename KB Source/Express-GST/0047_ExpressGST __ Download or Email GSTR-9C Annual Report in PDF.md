Process to Download the Filed GSTR-9C PDF

Follow these steps to download the filed GSTR-9C PDF for a specific financial year:

Step 1: Navigate to the client whose filed GSTR-9C PDF you wish to download. This action will open the Return Summary page.
Step 2: Select the relevant financial year for which the filed GSTR-9C PDF is required.
Step 3: Click the Download icon next to the GSTR-9C section.

      

Step 4: The system will automatically download and open the filed GSTR-9C PDF file.

By following these steps, you can effortlessly access the filed GSTR-9C PDF.

Process to Email the GSTR-9C Annual Report

To email the GSTR-9C Return PDF report, follow these steps:

Step 1: Navigate to the client whose GSTR-9C annual summary you wish to email. This action will open the Return Summary page.
Step 2: Click Prepare / Due / Filed in the GSTR-9C section.

      

Step 3: Go to the Review tab and select Email PDF to proceed.

      

Step 4: Input the recipient's email addresses in the Send Email dialog box.

      

Step 5: Click Send to dispatch the GSTR-9C PDF to the specified recipients.

By following these steps, you can efficiently email the GSTR-9C PDF directly through the ExpressGST platform.
