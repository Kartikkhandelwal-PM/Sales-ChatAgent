Sign-Up and Sign-In Assistance

Follow these steps to create an account and log in successfully:

Step 1: Access the Official Website
Visit our official platform and select the "TRY Now" option to access the sign-up page.

Depending on your requirement, use the respective application links:

GST Application: https://www.expressgst.com/

                        
Income Tax Return Filing Application: https://www.expressitr.com/

                        
TDS Return Filing Application: https://www.expresstds.com/

                        

Step 2: Register Your Account

Fill in the required details such as name, email address, mobile number, and any other requested information.

Click "SIGN UP" to proceed.

You will receive a One-Time Password (OTP) on your registered mobile number or email address.

Enter the OTP in the verification field to complete your registration.

Step 3: Log In to Your Account

Return to the website or application.

Select the "Sign In" option.

Enter your registered mobile number and password.

Click "Log In" to access your account.

Important Notes:

Password Security: Ensure your password is strong and remains confidential to protect your account.

Account Consistency: If you are using multiple products from the KDK Software and the same mobile number is registered, please use the same password across all platforms for a seamless experience.

We hope this guide helps you!
