GSTIN Search in ExpressGST

The GSTIN Search feature in ExpressGST allows users to retrieve comprehensive information about a registered GSTIN (Goods and Services Tax Identification Number). This tool is useful for verifying a GSTIN, accessing taxpayer details, and reviewing return filing status and frequency, along with the registered business address.

Key Benefits of the GSTIN Search Feature:

GSTIN Verification

Taxpayer Information

Return Filing Status and Frequency

Business Address

Steps to Search for a GSTIN in ExpressGST:

Step 1: Log in to the ExpressGST portal using your username and password. Once logged in, the Client Master page will display all available clients. From this list, select the relevant client for whom you wish to search a GSTIN by clicking on their name.

      

Step 2: This will take you to the Return Summary page, which displays the various GSTR filings (e.g., GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-9, GSTR-9C).
Step 3: To search for a GSTIN, click the "GSTIN Search" button.

      

Step 4: Enter the GSTIN you wish to search for in the search bar and press Enter.

Step 5: A window will open, providing all the necessary details for the searched GSTIN, including taxpayer information, return status, and business address.

      

By following these steps, you can easily and efficiently search for GSTIN details using the ExpressGST platform.
