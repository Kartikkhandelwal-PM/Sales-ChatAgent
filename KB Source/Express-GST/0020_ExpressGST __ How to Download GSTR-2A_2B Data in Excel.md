How to Download GSTR-2A/2B Data in Excel in ExpressGST

To download GSTR-2A/2B data in Excel format, follow these steps for an accurate and complete data download.

Step 1: Access the Reconciliation Section

Go to the Reconciliation section on the ExpressGST dashboard.

Select Books vs 2A/2B Annual from the reconciliation bar to access download options.

      

Step 2: Switch to Invoice-Wise Reconciliation

Click on Download 2A/2B Reco Data.

      
Choose the desired period and month.

      
To download data for the entire financial year, select the financial year and choose either Supplier & Invoice-wise 2A or 2B.

This approach ensures quick access to GSTR-2A/2B data with detailed, invoice- or supplier-wise organization.
