Steps to Remove Data and Reimport Books Data

If you need to remove GSTR-1 data (both Portal data and Software data) and reimport books data in ExpressGST, follow the steps below:

Remove Portal Data via the GST Portal

To remove data from the GST portal, complete the following steps:

Step 1: Select the Client in Client Master

Navigate to the Return Summary section.

Choose the client whose data needs to be downloaded or reset.

      

Step 2: Select the Month

Pick the relevant filing month.

Step 3: Choose Due/Overdue Option

Indicate whether the data is due or overdue based on the books data.

      

Step 4: Log in to the GST Portal

Click on Login to GST Portal and navigate to the return details.

      

Step 5: Click on the Reset Option

Select the correct return and click Reset to remove the data from the GST portal.

      

Step 6: Log Out of the GST Portal

After resetting the data, log out from the portal.

Remove Data from ExpressGST

Step 1: Start the Sync Process

After resetting the portal data, click on the Sync option in ExpressGST to start updating the system.

Step 2: Wait for Sync Completion

Allow the synchronization process to complete. This will ensure all outdated data is removed.

      

Note: The process may take some time.

Reimport Books Data into ExpressGST

Step 1: Reimport Correct Books Data

Use the Import Books Data feature in ExpressGST to upload the corrected data.

Step 2: Ensure Proper Details

Double-check that the correct client and month details are selected during the reimport process.

By following this process, you can successfully reset the data and re-import books data into ExpressGST.
