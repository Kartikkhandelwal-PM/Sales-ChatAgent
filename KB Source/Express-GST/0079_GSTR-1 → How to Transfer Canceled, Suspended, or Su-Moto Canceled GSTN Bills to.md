GSTR-1 → How to Transfer Canceled, Suspended, or Su-Moto Canceled GSTN Bills to B2CS

Follow these steps to transfer canceled, suspended, or Su-moto canceled GSTN bills to the B2CS section in ExpressGST:

Steps to Transfer GSTN Bills to B2CS:

Step 1: Import GSTR-1 Data

Import the GSTR-1 data into ExpressGST as usual.

Step 2: Save Data on the Portal

After importing, click on the "Save Data on Portal" option to ensure all details are saved.

      

Step 3: Upload to GSTN

Click on the "Upload on GSTN" button to push the data to the GSTN portal.

Step 4: Wait for Status Update

After uploading, the initial status might show as "Errored".

      

Step 5: Fix GSTN-Related Issues

If errors occur, click on "Click Here to Fix GSTN-Related Issues" to resolve discrepancies.

A table will display the GSTN bill's statuses, including canceled, suspended, or Su-moto canceled bills.

      
Step 6: Move Bills to B2CS

In the table, select the bills you want to transfer:

Select all bills, or

Choose individual bills.

Click on the "Move to B2CS" option to transfer the selected bills.

      

Step 7: Confirm the Action

After transferring the selected bills, click "Done" to confirm the action.

Step 8: Re-Upload to GSTN

Click the "Upload on GSTN" button again to upload the corrected data to the GSTN portal.

      

Step 9: Enter Captcha or OTP
During the final upload, enter the required Captcha or OTP to complete the process.

      

Step 10: Final Status Update

Once the data is successfully uploaded, the status will update to "Successful".

      
You can now proceed with filing your GSTR-1 return.

By following these steps, you can efficiently transfer canceled, suspended, or Su-moto canceled GSTN bills to the B2CS section in ExpressGST. This ensures the corrected data is uploaded successfully to GSTN, allowing you to file your return without errors.
