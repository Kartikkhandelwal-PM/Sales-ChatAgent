View Comparison of GSTR1, GSTR-3B and Books

To review the comparison between GSTR-1, GSTR-3B and Books in ExpressGST, follow these steps:

Step 1: Access GSTR-9 Status

Navigate to the Return Summary section.

Click on the GSTR-9 Status option to proceed.

      

Step 2: View Comparison Summary

Select the Comparison of GSTR 1, 3B and Books option to view the summary. This will provide a detailed comparison of the values reported in each form and your books.

      

      

      

Step 3: Download Comparison in Excel

To receive the comparison report via email in Excel format, click on the Email option, enter your email address, and send.

      

      

This process allows you to quickly access a comprehensive view of GSTR 1, 3B and Books data, ensuring accuracy in your GST reporting.
