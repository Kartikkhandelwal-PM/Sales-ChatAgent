NIC GSP Login Creation Process 

(Note: This is distinct from the NIC Portal Login credentials)

To facilitate E-Way Bill Reconciliation, users are required to create a new GST Suvidha Provider (GSP) account. Follow the steps below to complete the process efficiently:

Step-by-Step Guide to Create GSP Login

Step 1: Access the E-Way Bill System Portal

Navigate to the E-Way Bill system portal - https://ewaybill.nic.in/
Log in to the portal using your existing credentials.

      

Step 2: Initiate GSP Registration

Click on the “Registration” tab located in the left-hand menu.

From the dropdown menu, select “For GSP”.
      

Step 3: OTP Verification

The “Register your GST Suvidha Provider / ERP” window will appear.
Click “Send OTP”, input the OTP received on your registered mobile number, and then click “Verify OTP”.
      
      
      
Upon successful OTP verification, you will be redirected to the next step.

Step 4: Complete the GSP Registration

In the subsequent window, perform the following actions:

Select “Add/New” to begin the GSP Registration.
      
From the “GSP Name” dropdown, choose - "IRIS BUSINESS SERVICES LIMITED".
Enter a unique username for your GSP by appending any three alphabetic characters of your choice as a suffix (confirm by entering the same value twice).
      
Create a password (maximum of 15 characters) and confirm it by re-entering. This username and password will be used as NIC GSP credentials for future access.

Click “Add” to finalize the registration.

Step 5: Review GSP Details

After successfully creating your GSP, use the “View” option to verify and review the following details:

      
GSP Name

GSTIN

Username

Status

Type
Registered By

Registration Date

By following these steps, you will successfully generate NIC GSP login credentials, including the NIC GSP Username and Password, for seamless access to E-Way Bill services.
