# ExpressGST || Login to the GST Portal from ExpressGST

**Section:** Express GST

**Category:** Express-GST

**Source:** https://support.kdksoftware.com/portal/en/kb/articles/login-to-the-gst-portal-from-expressgst

**Tags:** expressgst, login to gst portal, express connector, login to gst, login, login to the gst portal, login to the gst portal from expressgst, login to the gst

---

Follow these steps to log in to the GST portal directly from the ExpressGST software:

Step 1: Select the Company

Open ExpressGST and choose the company from the Client Master list.
Click on the GSTN logo located at the top near the company name.

      

Step 2: Enter Captcha

A Portal window will appear asking for the Captcha code.

Enter the Captcha code and press Enter.

      

Step 3: Check for Express Connector

If a prompt appears regarding Express Connector, it indicates one of the following:The connector is not installed on your system.
The connector is outdated and needs to be updated to the latest version.

      
To proceed, click on "Click Here to Download" to download the connector.

Step 4: Install Express Connector 

After downloading the connector, run the installation file.
For detailed installation instructions - Click Here !
Step 5: Log in to the Portal
Once the Express Connector is installed, return to ExpressGST.

Use the Portal Login Logo option to log in to the GST portal.

By following these steps, you can seamlessly log in to the GST portal using the ExpressGST software. Ensure the Express Connector is installed for a smooth experience.
