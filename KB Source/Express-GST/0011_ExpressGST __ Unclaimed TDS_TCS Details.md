How to Check Unclaimed TDS/TCS Details in ExpressGST

In ExpressGST, you can view TDS/TCS details in an annual report, including unclaimed amounts. Follow these steps:
Select and Open the client profile for whom you want to view TDS/TCS details.
This opens the Return Summary page, displaying various GSTR returns (like GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-9, GSTR-9C).

      

To View TDS/TCS Unclaimed Report:
Choose the client to open their Return Summary page.

In the GSTR-3B section, On the right side of the screen, you will find the Offset Liability option.

      
Click on the TDS/TCS amount to open a summary where you can view details by:

Period-wise

Deductor-wise

Detailed-wise

      

      
This allows you to view and manage unclaimed TDS/TCS amounts efficiently for each client in ExpressGST.
