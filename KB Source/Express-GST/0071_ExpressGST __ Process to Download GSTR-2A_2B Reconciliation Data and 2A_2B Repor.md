# ExpressGST || Process to Download GSTR-2A/2B Reconciliation Data and 2A/2B Reports in Excel (DIY)

**Section:** Express GST

**Category:** Express-GST

**Source:** https://support.kdksoftware.com/portal/en/kb/articles/expressgst-process-to-download-gstr-2a-2b-reconciliation-data-and-2a-2b-reports-in-excel-diy

---

ExpressGST enables users to download GSTR-2A/2B data in Excel format for effective reconciliation and compliance. Data can be extracted either invoice-wise or supplier-wise, and reports are available on both an annual and monthly basis.

Step 1: Access the Reconciliation Module

Log in to your ExpressGST account.

From the dashboard, navigate to the Reconciliation section.

Select Books vs 2A/2B (Annual) to view the available download options.

Step 2: Download GSTR-2A/2B Reconciliation Data (Monthly & Yearly)

Click on Download Data option under Actions.

Specify the required period and month.

To download data for the entire financial year, select the applicable year.
In the download dialog box, choose the preferred format:
Supplier-wise
Invoice-wise
The selected data will be exported in Excel format for reconciliation purposes.

Step 3: Download GSTR-2A/2B Reports (Month-Wise)

Click on Download GSTR-2A/2B Report.

Select the relevant year and month.
Choose either the GSTR-2A Report or the GSTR-2B Report to initiate the download.

Key Advantages

Provides invoice-wise and supplier-wise data (annual and month-wise) for detailed reconciliation.
Supports GSTR-2A/2B (month-wise) report downloads.
Ensures accuracy, transparency, and compliance in GST record-keeping.
Simplifies reconciliation by offering direct Excel exports.

This structured process ensures that GSTR-2A/2B data can be seamlessly downloaded from ExpressGST, enabling efficient reconciliation and better compliance management.
