An Input Service Distributor (ISD) is a registered taxpayer that receives invoices for services utilized by its branches. The ISD distributes the tax paid—referred to as Input Tax Credit (ITC)—to the respective branches proportionally by issuing ISD invoices.

🔹 Although branches may have different GSTINs, they must operate under the same PAN as the ISD.

Books vs GSTR-6A Reconciliation
Select PAN - Go to the Client Master and select the applicable PAN.

Access Reconciliation Summary - Navigate to the Reconciliation Summary section and click on the Reco option.

Import Books Data - 
Click Import Books Data.

Choose the relevant month and upload the corresponding file.

Click Import to proceed.

Compare Data - Once imported, the Books Data can be directly compared with the GSTR-6A details.

Supplier-wise Reconciliation View - For a summarised, vendor-level comparison, view the Supplier-wise Reconciliation report.

ITC Distribution Process

Select Invoices -

Choose the invoice(s) for distribution.

Select the respective GSTIN or Branch Name.

Distribute ITC

Click Distribute to allocate ITC to the selected branch.

Adjust Input Amounts

Modify input amounts as per the type of service being distributed.

Split Input Amounts (Optional)

Use the Split option to divide the ITC among multiple branches.

Enter the desired values manually.

Multi-Branch Distribution 

A single invoice can be distributed across multiple branches:

Select the invoice

Choose multiple branches

Click Distribute
