How to Change Personal, Contact, Organization Information, and Login Password in ExpressGST

To update your personal, contact, and organization information, as well as your login password in ExpressGST, follow these steps:

1. Access Profile Settings

Log in to ExpressGST and click on the Profile Icon located in the top right corner of the screen.

      
Select Profile Settings from the dropdown menu to access your account settings.

      

2. Update Personal & Contact Information

In Profile Settings, go to Personal & Contact Information.

Click the pencil icon to edit details such as the contact person’s name, mobile number, and email address.

      
After entering the new information, click Save to apply the changes.

3. Change Organization Information

To update organization details, navigate to the Organization Information section.

Here, you can edit the organization name as required and save your changes.

      
Note: Organization / Firm Type Cannot be Changed.

4. Change Login Password

To reset your login password, select the Reset Password option.

Enter the new password as prompted and save it to complete the update.

      

These steps ensure that your personal, contact, organization information, and login credentials remain current in ExpressGST.
