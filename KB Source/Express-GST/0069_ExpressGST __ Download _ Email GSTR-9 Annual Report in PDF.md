Download Filed GSTR-9 PDF

To download the filed GSTR-9 PDF for a particular financial year, follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose the client for whom you want to download the Filed GSTR-9 PDF Report. This will open the Return Summary page.

Step 2: Select the specific financial year for which you want to download the filed GSTR-9 PDF.

Step 3: Click on the Download icon placed next to GSTR-9.

      

Step 4: After clicking the button, the software will automatically download and open the filed GSTR-9 PDF.

By following these steps, you can easily download the filed GSTR-9 PDF file.

Process to Email GSTR-9 Annual Report

To email the GSTR-9 Return PDF Report, follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose the client for whom you want to send the Annual Summary. This will open the Return Summary page.

Step 2: In the GSTR-9 section, click Prepare / Due / Filed.

      

Step 3: Navigate to the Review tab and click on Email PDF to send the GSTR-9 Report in PDF format.

      

      

Step 4: Enter the email addresses of the recipients in the Send Email dialog box.

      

Step 5: Click Send to email the GSTR-9 PDF report to the specified recipients.

By following these steps, you can easily email the GSTR-9 PDF to the necessary recipients directly from the ExpressGST portal.
